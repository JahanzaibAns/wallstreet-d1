Our Trademark Registration Services

Trademark Filing & Renewals

Whether you are looking to register a new trademark or you want to renew an old one, Alpha Trademark is your one-stop-shop. With a team of highly experienced trademark attorneys, Alpha Trademark has 10 years' experience of filing trademarks with USPTO.

Trademark Search

Trademark search is an important aspect of registering your trademark successfully. Before filing your application with the USPTO, we search for any conflicts within the USPTO database. If found, we get them resolved to ensure your application does not suffer any delays.

Complete Documentation Services

There are multiple legal documentation requirements when filing for a USPTO trademark such as a Trademark Statement of Use (SOU), Office Action Response, and the like. We take care of these legalities on your behalf to ensure your trademark registration is successful.
